team director
In charge of team selection, managing team competition data, checking conflict blocks, drafting teams, and general organization for all teams. May subordinate work to the co-officers when needed. This role can be considered advisory, but with executive authority on certain actions such as team rosters.

coord
This role will be in charge of making accurate and timely schedules based on the team rosters. They are also in charge of scheduling builds and reporting those times to the club advisors so they can register in time. They will support the team director whenever need be.

build
his role will be in charge of introducing builds to new members (i.e., giving tips, and working with them to suggest models, etc.), administering regular check-ins for build events, as well as securing facilities for testing, which should occur at least once in November, December, and January. Testing must be at a centralized location, and should be done as close to the actual rules as possible. It will also be proctored by other co-officers to reduce biases.
